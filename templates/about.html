About.

Hello, I'm Annie, and I'm the creative force behind "Brushstrokes." What began as a humble art blog has evolved into an art gallery and exhibition space right here in the heart of Glasgow.

Brushstrokes is where the pulse of local art beats. We are the canvas for Glasgow's talented artists, showcasing their incredible work and providing a platform for their creativity to flourish.

"Brushstrokes by Annie" is where it all started, the spark that ignited our artistic journey. It continues to inspire our gallery's spirit, where the digital world meets tangible artistry.

Our website is a window into the world of Glasgow's artistic soul. We invite you to join our vibrant community, connect with local artists, and stay updated on their latest creations available for your collection.

In this digital age, we blur the lines between the virtual and the tangible. Come be a part of our story, explore, create, and celebrate art with us.

Our Artists

Brian Lee

Brian Lee, a visionary artist, navigates the canvas with an unparalleled flair for abstract landscapes. His brush dances across the surface, weaving intricate tales of distant terrains and vibrant cityscapes. Through a kaleidoscope of colours, Brian captures the essence of diverse landscapes from around the world, transcending geographical boundaries in his mesmerizing creations. His artwork is a testament to a profound connection with nature and an exploration of the unseen, inviting viewers to embark on a visual journey through the imagination of a masterful painter.

Donald Romane

Donald Romane, an artistic virtuoso, skillfully navigates the spectrum from abstract expressions to intricately detailed portraits. His canvas becomes a mirror of human emotions, where every stroke whispers tales of joy, sorrow, and everything in between. With a palette that mirrors the complexity of emotions, Donald infuses life into his creations, inviting viewers to delve into the profound depths of his art. Through his adept brushwork and a rich interplay of colors, he not only paints portraits but also captures the intricate landscapes of the soul, establishing an emotional connection that resonates deeply with those who encounter his work.

Sophie Watson

Sophie Watson, a maestro in the art of botanical beauty, specializes in capturing the ethereal essence of flowers. Her delicate style of painting, adorned with soft strokes and gentle hues, breathes life into petals and leaves. Using the vibrant medium of acrylic paint, Sophie transforms her canvas into a garden of tranquility, where each bloom tells a tale of grace and serenity. With a meticulous touch, she unveils the intricate details of nature's wonders, inviting viewers into a world where the soft palette of colors becomes a soothing symphony for the eyes. Sophie's art is a celebration of the fleeting beauty found in every petal and the timeless elegance of the floral realm.

Paula McDonald

Paula McDonald, an abstract virtuoso, boldly wields her brush to create vivid and energetic paintings. Her distinctive style is characterized by bold, thick brushstrokes that dance across the canvas, infusing life and vibrancy into her creations. Paula's art is a celebration of spontaneity and joy, where each stroke is a playful exploration of color and form. With a fearless approach to abstraction, she invites viewers into a world of whimsy and excitement, where the canvas becomes a playground for the imagination. Paula's fun and dynamic paintings are a testament to her artistic freedom and a delightful journey into the boundless realm of abstract expression.

Ian Donald

Ian Donald, a masterful painter, delicately crafts Scottish landscapes with a unique blend of abstraction and soft beauty. His artistry transforms rugged terrains into poetic canvases, where the essence of Scotland is rendered with a gentle touch. Ian's brush dances across the canvas, creating abstract landscapes that evoke a serene and tranquil ambiance. Through beautifully soft strokes, he captures the mystique of the Scottish countryside, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in the ethereal beauty of the land. Ian's paintings are a testament to his ability to fuse abstraction with the serene spirit of nature, creating a harmonious and captivating visual experience.
